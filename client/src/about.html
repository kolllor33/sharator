1. Select a folder where your documents are which you want to share press the "Select folder" button.

2. If you like you can disable file so other people can access them just click on a filename to disable it.

3. When ready, click the "start sharing" button to share your files on your local network.

4. To download a file go to the given link in the application and click a filename to download.

5. If you want to download more than 1 file at a time just check the checkboxes and click the "download selected" button.
